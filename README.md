# 🌟 NEON FALL

**Hyper-casual neon-style falling game** with progression, upgrades, daily rewards and monetization.

## 🎮 About

NEON FALL is an addictive hyper-casual game where you control a neon character falling through an endless neon world. Bounce on platforms, avoid obstacles, collect coins, upgrade your character and enjoy daily rewards.

## ✨ Features

- 🎯 **Smooth controls** — Drag left/right + tap to boost
- 🪙 **Progression system** — Earn coins and spend them in the shop
- 🛍️ **Upgrades** — Improve magnet, score multiplier, extra lives and more
- 🎁 **Daily Rewards** — Login daily to earn coins and maintain your streak
- ⚡ **Power-ups** — Magnet, Score Boost, Slow Motion and Shield
- 🎵 **Chiptune music** — Toggleable retro soundtrack
- 📱 **Mobile optimized** — Works great on phones and tablets
- 💰 **Monetization ready** — Rewarded videos and interstitial ads integrated

## 🕹️ How to Play

- **Drag** left or right to move
- **Tap** the screen to boost upward
- Bounce on platforms to gain height
- Collect coins and avoid obstacles
- Use power-ups wisely
